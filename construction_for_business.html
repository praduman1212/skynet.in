Quality Check Mechanism

Mandatory Checks:

Checks that have to be performed at every site visit irrespective of the stage of the project.

No-Go Checks:

Stage wise checks where a project cannot proceed to the next stage if these quality checks have not passed.

Critical Checks:

Quality checks that are critical to the overall quality of the projects and site engineers have to make sure that these are cleared.
